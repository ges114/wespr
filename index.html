The Opportunity

Europe is building a new digital foundation

By 2026, every EU member state must provide citizens with a digital identity wallet under eIDAS 2.0. The Digital Euro is advancing toward pilot deployment. Yet no unified layer exists to connect identity, payments, and compliance.

W€spr fills that gap — building the connective tissue between the EUDI Wallet ecosystem and real-world transactions.

This is not a consumer app. It is infrastructure, built to the standards that Europe's digital economy requires.

01 / Regulation

Mandatory compliance window

EU member states face a hard deadline for eIDAS 2.0 wallet deployment. Compliance requires certified infrastructure providers.

02 / Market Gap

No sovereign alternative exists

Existing solutions rely on US-owned infrastructure. European sovereignty requirements demand a native alternative.

03 / Timing

First-mover certification advantage

EUDI Wallet certification creates durable barriers to entry. The window closes in 2026.
